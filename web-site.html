A Book Cover Design Trilogy for This is Criminal Podcast

"This is Criminal" is a podcast based on real-life crime events and hosted by American journalist, Pheobe Judge. After listening to a few episodes, I chose the three that took my interest: The Checklist, American Dream and Willie Bosket, and made book covers based on those episodes.
